About 60secs

Welcome to 60secs! 60secs is a social game where players must explain words to others without using the word itself in the explanation. The other players must try to guess the correct word within 60 seconds. 60secs is based on the popular classic game, 30 Seconds, but it has now been brought to the web, where anyone can play. It's much more convenient, and new words will be continuously added to reflect current trends and TikTok culture.
